Brazilian Blowout

Brazilian Blowout smoothing treatments use an innovative approach to improve the condition of your hair. These treatments create a protective protein layer around the hair shaft to eliminate frizz and smooth the cuticle.

This treatment:

  • Eliminates frizz
  • Imparts shine
  • Improves hair health
  • Reduces styling time
